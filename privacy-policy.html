Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Kiova ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Kiova mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register.
  • Images: Photos you upload to your wardrobe or saved pieces.
  • URLs: Product page links you share to save clothing items.
  • Payment information: Processed securely via PayPal. We do not store card details.
  • Usage data: Token usage and outfit generation history.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Kiova app and its features.
  • To process payments for token purchases.
  • To generate AI outfit suggestions using your wardrobe images.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL database) and Firebase Authentication. Images are hosted on third-party image hosting services. We take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – Authentication
  • Supabase – Database
  • PayPal – Payment processing
  • Google Gemini – AI outfit generation
  • ImgBB – Image hosting
  • ScraperAPI – Product image fetching

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Kiova is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
